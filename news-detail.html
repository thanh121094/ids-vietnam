Statement on GDPR
Piql welcomes the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as a significant step forward in strengthening the rights that EU individuals have over their data and creating a uniform data protection law across Europe.

Piql will comply with applicable GDPR regulations as a data processor when they take effect on 25th May 2018. We will work in conjunction with our clients, the data controllers, exploring opportunities within our service offerings to assist them to meet their GDPR obligations and help them to preserve their most valuable information securely.
